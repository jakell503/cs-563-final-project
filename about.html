My name is Jack Kelly I am a part time graduate student at Portland State University. I have been working as an actuary for the last 10 years at a group insurance company based out of Portland Oregon. Group insurance is when an employer or organization purchases coverage for their employees.

If you are curious about what an actuary does or would like to explore the profession I encourage you to visit The Society of Actuaries and the Bureau of Labor Statistics to learn more.

Most of my experience is in reserving where we estimate how much money an we must set aside today to pay all future claims, including those already reported and those that haven't happened yet but are expected.

Using historical data is instrumental in setting approprate reserves. We have large data sets of past claims that we use to understand the business and model future experience. We use many differnet technologies in our work ranging from mainframe and COBOL to more modern tools such as databricks and python.
